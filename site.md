# Keryx Solutions

> Keryx Solutions builds custom software, AI-powered tools, and scalable architectures for startups and growing businesses that need reliable, expertly engineered solutions.

## Who this is for

Keryx Solutions is best suited for organizations that need senior technical guidance, hands-on engineering support, or help turning AI experiments into maintainable systems and delivery practices.

## Core services

- Architecture modernization and codebase streamlining
- Frontend architecture and UX-minded product engineering
- Full-stack software delivery
- AI-assisted workflow design and practical AI integration
- Technical standards, mentoring, and delivery leadership

## Ideal outcomes

- Faster delivery with less avoidable complexity
- More maintainable code and clearer technical direction
- Better alignment between business goals, product decisions, and engineering execution
- AI used where it creates leverage rather than noise

## Technology focus

Common focus areas include JavaScript and TypeScript, React, Node.js, Python, WordPress, WooCommerce, Lightspeed Retail and eCommerce (formerly Ecwid), system architecture, developer experience, AI tooling, user experience, and practical PHP/Drupal modernization for existing systems.

## Founder

Keryx Solutions is led by Bala Bosch, Founder and Principal Engineer. Bala brings nearly three decades of software engineering experience, strong architecture depth, AI fluency, and a UX-informed approach to product delivery.
